Why FlowEngine?

a declarative cloud native process orchestration framework that covers offline, nearline, and online scenarios. It supports the whole process of data-driven system solution development and application management, and aims to help build strategic applications quickly and correctly.

Full scenarios coverage

Unified and consistent use and development experience is not only easy to start, but also can reduce the number of basic services, reduce the burden of operation and maintenance and the probability of errors

Easy to learn and use

Easy to use, declarative interface, low code interaction, complete the development and maintenance of business logic, reduce learning costs, and do not need to pay attention to the underlying details

Stable and reliable performance

Different sizes and different scenarios have been tested for many years, and can meet most business and technical requirements

Architecture governance

Concise, consistent and correct architecture can reduce the cost of team multi-role development collaboration and long-term system maintenance, and slow down the speed of system corruption

community & ecologicalization

Open architecture, hierarchical reuse, separation of development and use， Whether it's a big solution or a small component, you can get help through the community

CloudNative

Pure native architecture, elastic scaling, serviceless design, rapid deployment of new scenarios or migration of old scenarios

Features

  • Unified business/data flow orchestration (batch,stream,func)
  • Simple and consistent declarative api/sdk/gui
  • asol&job template reuse
  • Easy to save，migrate and rebuild
  • CloudNative& Elastic scale & Serverless
